His Story

Daniel's Journey

```

Mihail-Daniel Balaș was born in Constanța and attended the Faculty of Theology — paying nearly 20,000 euros for his studies, money earned working in England — with the dream of becoming a priest.

Ordained as a priest in the Archdiocese of Tomis, he served with passion in Ivrinezu Mic — a village of 100 souls 80 km from Constanța — without a salary and without a parish house. After 8 months of unanswered requests for support, he resigned.

He took to the open road and drove trucks across England for years, saving money for a new life. Simultaneously, he began speaking openly on social media about irregularities inside the Romanian Orthodox Church — and the world started listening.

In 2022 he was officially defrocked by the Archdiocese of Tomis. In 2023 he published his book "The Truth Behind the Cassock" and put down the steering wheel for good, dedicating himself entirely to online content creation.

At 29, with tens of millions of TikTok views, a Lamborghini Huracăn, and tattoos where the cassock used to be, Daniel is the Romanian voice that refuses to be silenced.

“Going to church doesn't make you a believer, just as sitting in a garage doesn't make you a car.”

— Daniel Balaș

2023 — The Author

The Book & Freedom

Published "The Truth Behind the Cassock" — a book about authenticity, freedom, and the secrets of the Church. Put down the truck keys for good and went full-time as a creator.
```
