His Journey

Born in Constanța, Romania, Daniel studied theology and worked in England to pay for his education. He was ordained as a priest and served in a small village for 8 months without salary or housing.

After facing silence from the church, he resigned and began speaking openly about issues within the Romanian Orthodox Church. He later drove trucks across Europe while building a strong online presence.

In 2023 he published his book "The Truth Behind the Cassock" and fully dedicated himself to honest content creation.
